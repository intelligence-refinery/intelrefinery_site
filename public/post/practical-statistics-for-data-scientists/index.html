Practical Statistics for Data Scientists

February 24 2020
A second edition, *Practical Statistics for Data Scientists: 50+ Essential Concepts Using R and Python* is scheduled for publication on June 9, 2020.


Who is this book for?

According to the preface:

This book is aimed at the data scientist with some familiarity with the R programming language, and with some prior (perhaps spotty or ephemeral) exposure to statistics.
Two goals underlie this book: 1) to lay out, in digestible, navigable, and easily referenced form, key concepts from statistics that are relevant to data science, and 2) to explain which concepts are important and useful from a data science perspective, which are less so, and why.

Impression

Strengths

Weaknesses
